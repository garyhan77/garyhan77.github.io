Italy - Europe
         
    
 

 

Venice is a beautiful city located in northeastern Italy, built on a series of islands connected by canals and bridges. Visitors can take a gondola ride down the Grand Canal, explore the historic St. Mark's Square and Basilica, and visit the iconic Rialto Bridge. The city is also known for its exquisite Venetian glass and delicious seafood cuisine, such as squid ink pasta and seafood risotto. Its narrow streets, stunning architecture, and rich history make it a must-visit destination for any traveller.
