Brazil - South America
         
    
 

 

Rio de Janeiro is a vibrant city located in Brazil, famous for its stunning beaches, colourful culture, and iconic landmarks. Visitors can soak up the sun at Copacabana or Ipanema beach, take a cable car up to the top of Sugarloaf Mountain for breathtaking views of the city, or explore the historic neighbourhood of Santa Teresa. The city is also known for its lively Carnival celebrations and samba dancing, as well as its delicious cuisine, which includes dishes such as feijoada and churrasco.
