Tulum is a stunning beach town located in the Yucatan Peninsula of Mexico. Known for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, powdery white sand beaches, and ancient Mayan ruins, Tulum is a popular destination for both adventure seekers and relaxation seekers alike. Visitors can explore the ancient ruins of Tulum, go snorkeling or scuba diving in the Caribbean Sea, or simply relax on the beach and soak up the sun.
